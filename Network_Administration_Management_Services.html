Network Administration Management Services

We are engaged in offering a wide range of Networking Management Services, such as designing, installation and maintaining of networks. Techsist services backed by a team of expert engineers, we are engaged in implementing networking contracts. We help in maximizing client's productivity by managing IT networking infrastructure. Our on-site network administrators will monitor your network infrastructure, ensure that your network functions at its optimum level and that effective performance & capacity planning practices are in place.

What we offer:

  • Managing Network Devices - Installation, Configuration and operation & maintenance of Load Balancers, Core Routers, Workgroup Routers, Edge Routers, L4 /L3 Switches, etc
  • Managing Network Performance - Monitor network thresholds, manage and administer network usage effectively. Detect network degradation before it becomes service affecting. Provide visibility of spare capacity and utilization trends.
  • Integrating, configuring the various OEM based products for monitoring the network traffic and increase operational excellence.
  • Structured Network Cabling services which will makes your network easy to maintain and which will increase your network reliability and speed.
  • WAN, Wired and wireless network equipment's - Installation, Configuration, O&M and AMC services
  • Network Optimization Services - Techsist Services employs best-of-breed caching, Quality of Service (QOS), compression and network monitoring/reporting technologies into a single solution that looks at bandwidth usage in its entirety and makes recommendations on how best to optimize it.
  • Network Fault Management - Optimizing the detection, performing root cause analysis, isolation and streamlining of alarms coupled to the problem resolution process. Accurate determination of fault and its location, results in improved meantime to repair at reduced cost.
  • Network security audit services- Ensure network security & isolate programs that are slowing the system. Free bandwidth for key applications. Understand and trace virus traffic. Engage in security & bug patch notification. Administer firewall up-to-dates.
  • Technical and Management Reporting - Event analysis, watch lists, recommendations and more.

Our Service Benefits:

  • Reduce total cost of ownership
  • Improve maintenance support and enhancement productivity
  • Improve service execution and availability
  • Enhance the performance, support and reliability of your investments by reducing risk and improving operational controls
  • Improve flexibility and scalability of your enterprise applications support model to adjust quickly to changes in business priorities and variations in demand.
